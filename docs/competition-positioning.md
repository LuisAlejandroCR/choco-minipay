# Competition Positioning

Reference competitor checked during repo split:

- URL: `https://monipay.xyz/minipay`
- Page title: `MoniPay - Gasless Multi-Chain Payments by Username`
- Meta description: `Send stablecoins by username. Zero gas. Non-custodial. Multi-chain across Base, BSC, Solana, Tempo, Ink and Celo.`

## What They Emphasize

- Username-based stablecoin payments.
- Gasless UX.
- Multi-chain reach.
- Non-custodial positioning.
- Broad chain coverage: Base, BSC, Solana, Tempo, Ink, Celo.

## Choco Differentiation

Choco should not compete as another generic send-by-username wallet.

Choco should compete as:

- MiniPay-native remittance concierge.
- Family-transfer and scheduled-transfer specialist.
- Celo-first, not chain-spread-thin.
- Phone/alias identity using ODIS and FederatedAttestations.
- USDC to KESm corridor with reviewable fees, receipts, retries, and support traces.
- ERC-8004-registered agent with provenance and future reputation.

## Hackathon Message

One sentence:

`Choco turns a MiniPay voice or text instruction into a scheduled family remittance with quote review, duplicate checks, Celo proof, and agent provenance.`

## Build Priority

Move faster by staying narrow:

1. MiniPay wallet detection.
2. USDC/KESm quote placeholder behind an interface.
3. Phone/alias recipient model behind an interface.
4. Send-now testnet hash.
5. Scheduled plan persistence.
6. Receipt and support trace.
7. ERC-8004 registration evidence.

Avoid multi-chain scope until the first corridor works.
