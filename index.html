Hackathon MVP

What gets built first.

The first version should prove the real work: understand text or voice, prepare the plan, record status, and show a receipt.

Focused scope

  • Lives inside Mini Apps for now
  • Accepts text or voice commands
  • One corridor: US to Kenya
  • One amount: 50,000 KES
  • One recurrence: day 1 each month
  • USDC to KESm quote
  • Retry on failure
  • Shareable onchain receipt
