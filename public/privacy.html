Privacy

What Choco expects to handle.

Last updated: June 8, 2026. Choco is a production-candidate MiniPay app for stablecoin remittance planning. This page explains the expected data surfaces before live transfer execution is enabled.

Data used by the app
  • Transfer instructions typed or spoken by the user.
  • Recipient aliases, schedule timing, amount, route, and receipt metadata.
  • MiniPay wallet address when the wallet provider exposes it.
Data boundaries

Wallet signing stays with MiniPay. Server-only provider keys and registry keys must stay outside public frontend variables.

Support records

Support issues may include receipt links, transaction hashes, route details, screenshots, and the contact path the user chooses to provide.

Production review

Final retention, analytics, compliance, and licensed-partner policies must be approved before live remittance flows launch.
