2. What Choco is

Choco is a non-custodial stablecoin remittance tool built on the Celo blockchain. It helps you:

  • Prepare USDC-to-KESm transfer actions for your wallet to sign
  • Set up recurring monthly schedules for cross-border transfers
  • View on-chain receipts of executed transfers

Choco prepares and proposes blockchain transactions. Your wallet (MiniPay, Privy, or any Celo-compatible wallet) must sign and approve every action. Choco cannot move funds without your explicit wallet signature.

ChocoGateway, our on-chain contract, temporarily holds a single locked run's USDC during a scheduled transfer window. This lock is user-initiated, wallet-approved, and can be reclaimed by the user at any time via the "Reclaim funds" action.
